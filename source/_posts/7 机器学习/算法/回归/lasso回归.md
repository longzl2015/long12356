- 假设函数: $h_{\theta}=\theta_{0}x_{0}+\theta_{1}x_{1}+…+\theta_{n}x_{n}$
- 损失函数: $J(\theta)=\frac{1}{2m}\sum_{i=1}^{m}(h_{\theta}(x^i)-y^i)^2+\lambda\sum_{j=1}^{n}|\theta_{j}|$
- 目标: $\min J(\theta_{0},\theta_{1},\theta_{2}….\theta_{n})$
